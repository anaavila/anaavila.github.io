For Girls in Tech

Useful information for girls in technology fields

Grace Hopper Celebration of Women in Computing

The world's largest gathering of women technologists where you can find opportunities such as career fairs, job interviews, listen to keynotes from women technologists, meet other women like you, and more. Visit their website for all the information inlcuding how to get a scholarship to attend this conference.

#GHC2018 September 26 - September 28 in Houston, Texas.

Girls Who Code

Organization founded with the mission to close the gender gap in technology. You can find free summer programs, and after-school clubs for teen girls. Visit their website for more information and locations near you.
