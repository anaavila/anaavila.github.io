Eiffel Tower Cardboard

Autodesk: AutoCAD
Materials: Lights, Cardboard, Paint, Glue, Sissors, etc.

I made a cardboard Eiffel Tower to decorate a school dance themed "A Night in Paris".
Final product height is about 6.8 ft.

Step #1

Trace an Eiffel Tower using AutoCAD.

Divide the Eiffel Tower on rectangles (US letter size), export them as pdf, and print them. Then cut the faces and glue them to be able to trace them on cardboard.



To test the procedure, first make a small Eiffel tower.

This shows an example of a pdf from the small pdf version!

Step #2

To paint the tower, I first painted it all black. Once the black paint dried, I painted yellow on top. I made these patterns with hardpaper and laminated them to spray the Eiffel tower with yellow!

Step #3

Once the paint dried, I placed led strings all around and it was ready.

Step #4

Decorate a space and enjoy!

This project was very fun and interesting since I learned that I can use AutoCAD to do a craft project.
