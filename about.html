About Me

Hello!

My name is Ana Avila. I got my B.S. in Computer Science from CSU Stanislaus.

I am always willing to collaborate on different Computer Science topics, but I am passionate about two specific areas. I like to work on web development projects, and data manipulation. I enjoy the process of creating websites from designing wireframes up to writing the code in JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. Currently, I focus on front-end development, because I like to create responsive designs where the website adapts to different devices and screen sizes.

I also like to make programs for data manipulation using Python. I like to combine the potential of Python with web development, since I can write simple programs to populate data in a website, and to compress HTML and CSS code to make the website load faster.

In my spare time, I enjoy making illustrations using Adobe CC. I create new content as well as content that adapts to existing designs. My considerations when designing content, is to make it user friendly and simple to understand so that users have a good experience while being informed. I focus on making content for different platforms and formats (printed designs, social media, videos) so that it is accessible for a variety of users' preferences.

One of the things I value in life is education (schools, online learning resources, and people who share their knowledge with others). Because of that, I enjoy sharing with others what I have learned. I volunteered in a high school by tutoring students (high school level) basic computer programming concepts. I enjoy this type of experiences since students are able to learn something new, and I also learn new perspectives from them.
